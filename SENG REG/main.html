How to register for class each semester
A complete step by step procedure

01

Type in umis.babcock.edu.ng in the address bar of your browser and click enter.

02

a.Click on the student menu
b.Login with your username (Matric Number or Application ID for new students) and password obtained from your school officer.

03

In the welcome page, click on Commence Registration in the left column.

Then when the next page loads, select Commence Registration.

04

On the next page, select your Meal plan from the list of options using the check boxes and then click on the Action button below the selection field.

05

Afterwards, select your Worship center from the list of options using the check boxes and then click on the Action button below the selection field.

06

Click on Special Selection in the left column and select General Study Division for GEDS courses using the green Action button. Other courses can be found in each Department/Schhol's option tab (Course Registration is advised to be done before Financial Registration but not submitted until when recommended to do so during the school session).

07

After reviewing that you have picked the right courses in the Selected Course List area, click on Submit Course Form option in the left side column, then after proper review again, click on Submit Courses. (This is advised to be done only when recommended during the school session)
