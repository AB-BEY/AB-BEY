How to make payments and navigate the Finance section
A complete step by step procedure

01

For Financial Registration, ensure that your fees have been paid to your etranzact account through the number given in your Student's Profile. Click on Personal Details to find your Etranzact Card Number on the page. For 100 level students, payments will be made using Application ID numbers until Matriculation numbers and Etranzact card numbers have been given.

02

After getting your Etranzact Number, click on Financial Registration tab then click on Finance Statement to see the preview of your previous payment status; this option usually refreshes your payment details in the Current Charges Tab. Afterwards, click on Select Payments to choose the option you desire or what you have been instructed to pay. (The Acceptance Fee is only paid once by new students). Click on the Action button when done.

03

Afterwards, you will be automatically redirected to your Current Charges page to see your financial details. The important thing to note is the Fees to Pay. It is calculated based on your department, course, level, meal option and hall option. Ensure the amount (usually plus an addition of #500 for service charge) is paid into the Etranzact Card number in the Bank. After about an hour of confirmed payment with the bank receipt, click on the GO icon.

04

In the next page after clicking on the "GO" icon, click Request For Financial Approval. Ensure you have selected your right courses with the acceptable number of units for your level or else you won't be able to access the next page after clicking on the Request button.

05

After getting confirmation to make payment, click on the Make Payments tab, then click on the "eTranzact" Logo on the next page.

06

A new Tab on your browser will be automatically opened which will be the etranzact Payment Portal for Babcock where the Amount to Pay and your Card Balance will be shown. The Card Balance reflects the amount paid in the Bank using your etranzact card number. Ensure that Card Balance more than the Amount To Pay or else the payment will not be successful. Then click on Pay. You will get a notification if the transaction succeeds or not. If all instructions were appropriately, it definitely will be successful. Click on the Proceed option given in that notification.

07

Afterwards, go back to your Current Charges page and ensure that your Fees To Pay shows "0". Wait for a couple of minutes to get Finance Approval (The indication is "Yes") to be able to print your Receipt. If you made any mistake and weren't able to make a successful payment, ensure you click on the Open Finance button to enable you process your Financial Registration again. To print your receipt click on the GO button.

08

Then, in the next page, click on the Receipt tab. When the online receipt is displayed, scroll down and click on the PDF button to download it for printing. It is advisable to print at least three copies. You are advised to download and print the receipt using a laptop or desktop due to the web layout of the UMIS Portal.
